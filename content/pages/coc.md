# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

DevCongress is a community built on respect, collaboration, and the shared belief that builders across Africa deserve a space to grow together. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**We expect community members to:**

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy and kindness to fellow members

**We do not tolerate:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sustained disruption of community discussions or events
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all DevCongress spaces — including our Slack community, events, meetups, online forums, and any other space where you represent DevCongress or its community.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the DevCongress team at **hello@devcongress.org**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Community leaders may take any action they deem appropriate, including a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the community.
